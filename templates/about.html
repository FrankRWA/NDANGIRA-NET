What is Serivasi?
Serivasi is Rwanda's first dedicated platform connecting households with trusted local workers and affordable housing. We put affordable house commissioning at the center - because decent housing is a right, not a luxury.
Our mission
To create dignified, visible work for Rwanda's everyday service workers while saving employers time and building trust through transparency.
